About Docotela X

Docotela X is a luxury, art-driven fashion house creating limited, handcrafted collections inspired by the feeling of freedom. Each piece is an emotional story translated into fabric and art.

Our Philosophy

Emotion first. Craft second. Exclusivity always. Our collections are intentionally small to preserve the art-like value of each piece and to honor the artists who handcraft them.

How We Work

We collaborate with young artists for each collection, giving them creative freedom and spotlight while maintaining the cinematic, luxurious Docotela X identity.
