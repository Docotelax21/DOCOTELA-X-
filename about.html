Docotela X

We build for the few who prefer the slow, considered life. Docotela X began as the stubborn pursuit of freedom — freedom from the constant acceleration of trends, freedom to make things slowly and beautifully.

Our craft is small-batch and hand-finished. Each stitch is a choice; each seam, a conversation between maker and material. We are not loud. Our garments are private ceremonies — pieces that age with you, not against you.

Our story started with an old VW, a bare shell and six years of quiet work. It taught us two things: consistency turns doubt into proof, and the things worth making take time. That patience became the backbone of the brand — we make fewer things, but we make them true.

Our promise is to protect the feeling of freedom — in choice, in wear, and in living. We invite you to move slowly with us.
