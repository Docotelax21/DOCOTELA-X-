About Docotela X

Docotela X is a feeling — a quiet rebellion translated into cloth, metal and paper. We make objects that demand patience. Each stitch, fold and mark is a conversation between discipline and escape.

We began as a small studio with a stubborn belief: that clothing could be an archive of memory and freedom. We built, failed, redesigned and kept the few ideas that carried truth. The work is deliberate, limited, and not eager to please. It is made for those who recognise the value of solitude and the subtlety of detail.

Every collection is a chapter — some loud, some whispering — but all honest. We work with ten artists and handcraft each limited piece to hold a story. We are committed to craft as practice, not as spectacle.
