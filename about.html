About Docotela X

A Maker's Quiet Rebellion

Docotela X began as a resistance to speed and conformity. Every fold, seam and finish is an argument for time — slow, deliberate, human. We craft collections as fragments of a life, each collection a chamber of memory and motion.

We failed many times. We rebuilt a 1978 Beetle by hand across six years to prove a point — consistency matters. That project became proof that vision and patience can make the impossible look inevitable. That ethos is sewn into every piece.

Limited runs. Handcrafted details. Quiet confidence.

We believe true luxury is freedom — the freedom to choose who we are and how we move. Docotela X is an invitation to that feeling.
