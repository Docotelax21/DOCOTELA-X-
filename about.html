About Docotela X

Docotela X is the feeling of freedom translated into cloth, metal and light — a careful tension between discipline and escape. Each collection is treated like a small scene: private, limited, patient.

Our Philosophy

We pursue restraint as expression. Designs arrive through subtraction: each seam removed is a quiet decision. We keep runs intentionally small so each piece arrives with the breath of the artist who made it.

The Journey

From a 1978 VW Beetle shell to hand-stitched garments and midnight workshops — the studio formed by refusal. Investors said no, then watched. We learned to keep working. The car, rebuilt over six years, proved the consistency of a vision: finish what you start and let the work speak.

Craft & Rarity

Each drop is limited to a number of handcrafted pieces. We partner with ten young artists to handcraft each release — a promise that every object carries a visible mark of its maker.
